
            <p>section 7: <h2 class="text-danger">Rules for pedestrians</h2></p>
            <h3 class="text-info">Rules for pedestrians</h3>
            <p>If there is a footpath, you <span class="text-danger">must</span> use it.</p>
            <p>If there is no footpath, you <span class="text-danger">must</span> walk as near as possible to the right-
                    hand side of the road (facing oncoming traffic).
                    </p>
            <p>Do not walk more than two abreast. If the road is narrow or carries heavy
                    traffic, you <span class="text-primary">should</span> walk in single file.
                    </p>
            <p>You <span class="text-primary">should</span> always wear bright and hi-viz clothing during the day and</p>
            <p>reflective clothing at night when walking outside built-up areas. You <span class="text-primary">should</span> always carry a torch when walking at night time. You <span class="text-primary">should</span> always be aware of other road users.</p>
